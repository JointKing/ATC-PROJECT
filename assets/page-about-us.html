About Us

    Who We Are ?

    Years of experience in Logistics services

    ATC Freight & Shipping, is a full service organization that handles cargo by sea and air from anywhere in the world to Jamaica. Our primary markets are the Panama, China, Curacoa, Canada, United Kingdom, and the United States. Our warehousing consolidation service puts international shipping at your fingertips.

    We are experienced in accommodating any size and amount of cargo, and have extensive expertise in palletizing and crating shipments as well as stuffing and stripping containers. We can assure you that full loads, and even the smallest of shipments will be packed or consolidated into containers, or air freighted with the greatest of care. We strive to meet the needs of all of our clients and through our forwarding agents worldwide.
